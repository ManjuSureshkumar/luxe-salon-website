Frequently Asked Questions

How long is my membership valid?
Your membership is valid for the duration of the selected plan: monthly, quarterly, or yearly. It automatically renews unless canceled.
Can I cancel my membership?
Yes, you can cancel anytime before the next billing cycle.
Do you offer refunds?
Refunds are not available once the membership is activated.
What are the rules for using my membership benefits?
Benefits can be used during the active period and are subject to salon terms and availability.
